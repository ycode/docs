# Billing

Ycode Cloud uses Stripe for subscription management and payments. Each project has its own subscription.

## Subscribing

Subscribe to Ycode Cloud from your project's settings. Once subscribed, your project gets access to all platform features including custom domains, publishing, and priority support.

## Managing Your Subscription

Access billing management from the dashboard by clicking the **⋮** menu on a project card and selecting **Billing details**. This opens the Stripe Customer Portal where you can:

- **View invoices** and payment history
- **Update payment methods** — Change your credit card or payment details
- **Change plan** — Switch between monthly and annual billing
- **Cancel subscription** — End your subscription at the end of the current billing period

<Callout type="info" title="Migrating from Legacy">
If you migrated from Ycode Legacy, your existing subscription and pricing carry over automatically. You keep your current price and get all features of the new Ycode.
</Callout>

## Subscription Status

Your project's subscription status is shown on the dashboard card:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Active** | Subscription is current and the project is fully functional |
| **Trialing** | Free trial period before the first charge |
| **Past due** | Payment failed; update your payment method to avoid interruption |
| **Cancelled** | Subscription ended; project may have limited functionality |
