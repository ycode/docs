# Ycode Cloud

Ycode Cloud is the managed hosting platform for Ycode. Sign up, create projects, and start building — no server setup required. Ycode Cloud handles hosting, deployments, SSL, and infrastructure.

## Dashboard

The dashboard is your home in Ycode Cloud. It shows all the projects in the active workspace as cards with thumbnails, names, and URLs. Click a project to open it in the editor.

From the dashboard you can:

- **Create new projects** — Start from scratch or choose a template
- **Switch workspaces** — Use the workspace switcher in the header to move between your workspaces
- **Open projects** — Click a card to launch the editor
- **Manage billing** — Access the Stripe customer portal from the project menu
- **Delete projects** — Remove projects you no longer need

[Dashboard documentation](/docs/cloud/dashboard)

## Workspaces

Workspaces organise your projects. Every account has a personal workspace by default, and you can create additional workspaces to group projects by client, topic, or stage.

[Workspaces documentation](/docs/cloud/workspaces)

## Account

Manage your profile, password, and account settings.

[Account documentation](/docs/cloud/account)

## Billing

Subscribe to Ycode Cloud and manage your subscriptions through Stripe. Each project has its own subscription — free projects live forever with the full editor and CMS; upgrade per project to unlock paid features.

[Billing documentation](/docs/cloud/billing)

## Teams

Invite team members to collaborate on your projects with role-based access. Team membership is scoped per project.

[Teams documentation](/docs/cloud/teams)

## Help

The Ask Ycode chat bubble lives in the bottom-right of every authenticated dashboard page. Conversations follow your account across browsers and devices, and the current page is included with each message so answers can reference what you're looking at.
