# Account Settings

Manage your profile, password, and account from the Account page. Access it from the user menu in the top-right corner of the dashboard.

## Sign Up and Sign In

You can create an account and sign in using:

- **Email and password** — Standard registration with email verification
- **Google** — One-click OAuth sign-in with your Google account

## Profile

- **Full name** — Your display name, shown to team members on shared projects
- **Avatar** — Upload a profile photo (max 2 MB, image files only)

## Password

Change your password from the Account page:

1. Enter your **current password**
2. Enter and confirm your **new password**
3. Click **Save**

## Deleting Your Account

You can permanently delete your Ycode Cloud account from the Account page. This action:

- Deletes all projects you own (and their data)
- Removes your membership from projects you collaborate on
- Deletes your authentication account

<Callout type="warning" title="Active subscriptions">
You cannot delete your account while you own projects with active paid subscriptions. Cancel your subscriptions first, then delete the account.
</Callout>
