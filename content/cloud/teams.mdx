# Teams

Collaborate on projects by inviting team members with role-based access. The team model depends on the [workspace](/docs/cloud/workspaces) the project belongs to:

- **Personal workspace** — Each project manages its own team. Invitations and roles are scoped to a single project.
- **Agency workspace** — A single workspace-wide team spans every project inside. See [Workspace Team](/docs/cloud/workspaces#workspace-team) for the workflow specific to agency workspaces.

The rest of this page covers per-project teams (the model used in personal workspaces).

## Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control over the project, billing, and team. Can delete the project and manage subscriptions |
| **Admin** | Can edit the project and manage team members |
| **Editor** | Can edit pages, content, and design but cannot manage team settings or billing |

## Inviting Team Members

Invite collaborators from within the project settings:

1. Open the project in the editor
2. Go to project settings
3. Add a team member by email and assign a role

The invitee receives an email with a link to accept the invitation. If they don't have a Ycode Cloud account yet, they can sign up during the acceptance flow.

## Accepting an Invite

When you receive a team invitation:

1. Click the link in the invitation email
2. Sign in or create an account if you don't have one
3. The project appears on your dashboard with your assigned role badge

## Managing Team Members

Admins and owners can view team members and pending invitations from the project settings. You can change roles or remove members at any time.

<Callout type="info" title="Multiple projects">
You can be a member of multiple projects with different roles. Your dashboard shows all projects you have access to — both projects you own and projects you've been invited to, across every workspace you belong to.
</Callout>
